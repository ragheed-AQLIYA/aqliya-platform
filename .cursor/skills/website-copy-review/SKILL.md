# Website Copy Review Skill

## Review Criteria

1. Is AQLIYA positioned as a governed institutional intelligence platform?
2. Is Arabic copy sharp, institutional, and specific?
3. Are claims evidence-based?
4. Are CTAs clear and pointing to real routes?
5. Are product boundaries clear?
6. Are inactive products presented honestly?
7. Is there any fake production/security/compliance claim?

## Rewrite Style

- direct
- enterprise-grade
- Arabic-first when public Saudi/GCC facing
- no hype
- proof > opinion

## Reference

- `docs/official/aqliya-vision-v1.1.md`
- `docs/source-of-truth/PRODUCT_STATUS_MATRIX.md`
- `AGENTS.md` section 20 (commercial truthfulness)

## Final Output

- page/route reviewed
- issues found (P0/P1/P2)
- suggested rewrites (if asked)
- broken or misleading links

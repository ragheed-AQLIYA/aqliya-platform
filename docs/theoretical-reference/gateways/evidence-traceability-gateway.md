# Evidence Traceability Gateway

## Purpose
Maps the conceptual treatment of evidence traceability, risk boundaries, and human review judgment to the approved legacy doctrine that governs how AQLIYA sources, tracks, and assures evidence across workflows and audit engagements.

## What This Topic Means Inside AQLIYA
Evidence traceability is the backbone of AQLIYA's auditability guarantee — every recommendation, finding, and decision must be linked to verifiable evidence with known provenance, lineage, and confidence. This ensures that no output exists without a defensible chain back to source data and human review.

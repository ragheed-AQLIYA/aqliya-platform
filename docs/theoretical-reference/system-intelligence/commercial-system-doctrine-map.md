# Commercial System — Doctrine Map

Maps commercial and go-to-market positioning to doctrinal foundations in Part 14 (Commercialization) and Part 19 (Narratives).

## Commercial Position Mapping

| Commercial Position | Doctrine Source | Evidence Requirement | Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Value-Based Pricing | Part 14 §14.2 — Value Attribution | Pricing model must cite quantified client outcomes attributable to AQLIYA | Pricing cannot exceed documented value delta; must be peer-reviewed |
| Competitive Differentiation | Part 19 §19.3 — Narrative Integrity | All competitive claims must reference auditable product evidence or third-party benchmarks | Claims must be disclaimed if based on projections rather than measured results |
| Client Success Story | Part 19 §19.5 — Case Narrative | Narrative must include verified outcome metrics, client consent, and audit reference | No projection of future results; must include "past performance" disclaimer |
| Partnership / Channel GTM | Part 14 §14.4 — Channel Governance | Partner must sign data trust agreement; revenue share terms filed on-chain | Partner cannot modify product claims without re-review; quarterly compliance check |
| Free Trial / Pilot | Part 14 §14.6 — Trial Governance | Trial scope, duration, and success criteria must be documented pre-trial | No conversion without explicit governance sign-off on trial outcomes |
| Enterprise License | Part 14 §14.8 — Enterprise Terms | License must reference doctrine version; renewal conditioned on continued compliance | Volume discount must not compromise audit scope or independence |
| Thought Leadership Content | Part 19 §19.1 — Narrative Authority | Content must cite doctrine principles; claims attributable to publicly referenced evidence | No unsubstantiated market claims; reviewed by Doctrine Steward |
| ROI Calculator | Part 14 §14.3 — Outcome Projection | Calculator must display confidence intervals and data sources for each input | Cannot guarantee results; must display "projection, not guarantee" notice |

## Commercial Evidence Chain

```
Doctrine (Part 14, 19)
    │
    ▼
Commercial Position ──► Evidence Requirement ──► Boundary
                                  │
                                  ▼
                      AuditOS attestation or third-party verification
                                  │
                                  ▼
                      Client-facing material with disclaimers
```

## Governance Implications

- All commercial materials referencing doctrine must be pre-approved by a Doctrine Steward
- Evidence requirements are minimums; Commercial Officer may impose stricter requirements per deal tier
- Boundary violations trigger a hold on the commercial activity until remediated
- Quarterly commercial audit reconciles all active positions against current doctrine version

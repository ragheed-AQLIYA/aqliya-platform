# Part 15 — Responsible Intelligence

## Purpose
Establish professional and ethical boundaries for AI in audit and financial intelligence. Define what AQLIYA will and will not do.

## Why It Matters
Audit is a regulated profession with legal liability. AI that crosses the boundary from assistance to autonomy creates unacceptable professional risk for clients and AQLIYA.

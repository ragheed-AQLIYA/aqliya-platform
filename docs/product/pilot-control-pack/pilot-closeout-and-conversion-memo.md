## Conversion Recommendation

| Scenario                     | Recommendation              | Required Actions                           |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| All criteria met             | ✅ **Convert to Paid**      | Prepare proposal, service agreement        |
| All except minor criteria    | ✅ **Convert with caveats** | Address concerns in paid scope             |
| Significant criteria not met | 🔄 **Extend pilot**         | Define new scope, timeline, criteria       |
| Governance/security fail     | ❌ **Do not convert**       | Close pilot. Document lessons.             |
| Customer declines            | ❌ **No conversion**        | Close pilot. Data deletion within 30 days. |

### Final Recommendation

☐ Convert to Paid
☐ Extend Pilot — New Scope Required
☐ Stop — Do Not Convert

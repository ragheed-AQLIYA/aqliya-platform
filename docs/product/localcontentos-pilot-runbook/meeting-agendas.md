# LocalContentOS — Meeting Agendas

**Status:** Pilot Operations Guide — not software
**Purpose:** Structured agendas for all pilot meetings

---

## 1. Discovery Call — 30 Minutes

**Objective:** Qualify the customer, understand their local content needs, and determine if a pilot is appropriate.

**Attendees:** AQLIYA Lead, Customer Decision Maker

**Preparation Required:**

- Review ICP and use cases (from Discovery Pack)
- Review pilot offer (from Discovery Pack)
- Identify which ICP segment the customer fits

### Agenda

| Time      | Item                                                                                                                              | Owner       |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| 0:00–0:05 | **Introduction** — AQLIYA overview, LocalContentOS concept                                                                        | AQLIYA Lead |
| 0:05–0:12 | **Customer context** — How does customer currently measure local content? What systems do they use? What challenges do they face? | Customer    |
| 0:12–0:20 | **Pilot concept** — What a pilot involves (analyst-led, template-based, no software)                                              | AQLIYA Lead |
| 0:20–0:25 | **Q&A** — Answer customer questions, address concerns                                                                             | Both        |
| 0:25–0:30 | **Next steps** — If interested, schedule kickoff. What customer needs to prepare.                                                 | Both        |

**Decisions Needed:**

- Customer interest in proceeding to kickoff
- Tentative pilot scope (reporting period, category)

**Follow-up Actions:**

- Send pilot offer overview
- Schedule kickoff meeting

---

## 2. Pilot Kickoff — 60 Minutes

**Objective:** Align on pilot scope, process, deliverables, timeline, and expectations.

**Attendees:** AQLIYA Lead, AQLIYA Analyst, Customer Sponsor, Customer SME (Procurement/Local Content)

**Preparation Required:**

- Completed intake checklist
- Customer instructions and data templates prepared
- Kickoff deck prepared

### Agenda

| Time      | Item                                                                                                         | Owner          |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------- |
| 0:00–0:05 | **Welcome and introductions**                                                                                | Both           |
| 0:05–0:15 | **Pilot scope and objectives** — Reporting period, category, expected data volume, what success looks like   | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:15–0:25 | **Data requirements walkthrough** — Required files, templates, evidence folder structure, naming conventions | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:25–0:30 | **Regulatory boundary** — What this pilot is and is not. No regulatory submission. No software.              | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:30–0:40 | **Process and timeline** — 17-step operating procedure, meeting cadence, deliverables by week                | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:40–0:45 | **Roles and responsibilities** — Who does what, data owner, approval owner                                   | Both           |
| 0:45–0:50 | **Data sensitivity** — Agree on sensitivity classification and handling                                      | Both           |
| 0:50–0:55 | **Q&A**                                                                                                      | Both           |
| 0:55–1:00 | **Next steps and deadlines** — Data submission deadline, scheduled recurring meetings                        | Both           |

**Decisions Needed:**

- Confirm pilot scope and timeline
- Confirm data owner and approval owner
- Agree on data sensitivity handling
- Confirm meeting schedule

**Follow-up Actions:**

- Send confirmed timeline and meeting invites
- Send data request with templates (within 24 hours)
- Customer to prepare data by agreed deadline

---

## 3. Data Intake Review — 45 Minutes

**Objective:** Review received data, confirm readiness for analysis, address gaps.

**Attendees:** AQLIYA Analyst, Customer SME

**Preparation Required:**

- AQLIYA analyst completes steps 4–6 (Data Receipt, File Inventory, Completeness Review)
- Analyst prepares gap summary

### Agenda

| Time      | Item                                                                                         | Owner          |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| 0:00–0:05 | **Recap of data submission** — What was received, file count, format check                   | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:05–0:15 | **Completeness review** — Required fields, missing data, format issues                       | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:15–0:25 | **Gap discussion** — Review each gap, customer provides context or missing data              | Customer SME   |
| 0:25–0:35 | **Evidence review** — Evidence files received, any missing or unclear items                  | Both           |
| 0:35–0:40 | **Revised timeline** — Adjust analysis timeline based on data readiness                      | Both           |
| 0:40–0:45 | **Next steps** — Analyst proceeds with classification and review. Outstanding items tracked. | Both           |

**Decisions Needed:**

- Which gaps to resolve before proceeding vs. deferring
- Deadline for any missing data

**Follow-up Actions:**

- Customer to provide missing items by agreed date
- Analyst begins classification review

---

## 4. Findings Review — 60 Minutes

**Objective:** Present classification results, exceptions, and draft findings to customer management.

**Attendees:** AQLIYA Lead, AQLIYA Analyst, Customer Sponsor, Customer SME, Other stakeholders

**Preparation Required:**

- AQLIYA analyst completes steps 7–13 (Vendor review through Findings drafting)
- Findings presentation deck prepared
- Classification summary report prepared
- Evidence coverage report prepared

### Agenda

| Time      | Item                                                                                                        | Owner          |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| 0:00–0:05 | **Summary of pilot progress** — What was reviewed, data coverage, timeline status                           | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:05–0:15 | **Classification results** — Local vs. non-local breakdown, vendor classification map, spend classification | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:15–0:20 | **Evidence coverage** — Evidence confidence distribution, coverage gaps                                     | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:20–0:35 | **Key findings** — Top 3–5 findings with evidence gaps, financial impact, recommendations                   | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:35–0:50 | **Management discussion** — Customer reactions, context, decisions on findings                              | Customer       |
| 0:50–0:55 | **Unresolved items** — Items requiring further data or management decision                                  | Both           |
| 0:55–1:00 | **Next steps** — Final meeting scheduling, additional data needed for decision                              | Both           |

**Decisions Needed:**

- Management response to each critical and high finding
- Classification overrides requiring approval
- Confirmation of unresolved items

**Follow-up Actions:**

- Customer to provide management decisions on findings
- Analyst prepares final pilot summary and decision memo

---

## 5. Final Pilot Decision Meeting — 60 Minutes

**Objective:** Present the final pilot assessment, recommendation, and agree on next steps.

**Attendees:** AQLIYA Lead, AQLIYA Analyst, Customer Decision Maker, Customer Sponsor

**Preparation Required:**

- AQLIYA analyst completes steps 14–16 (Management review through Pilot summary)
- Decision memo prepared
- Decision meeting deck prepared
- Commercial offer prepared (if proceeding)

### Agenda

| Time      | Item                                                                                             | Owner          |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------- |
| 0:00–0:05 | **Pilot recap** — Scope, timeline, data received, what was accomplished                          | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:05–0:15 | **Pilot outcomes** — Key metrics, classification summary, evidence coverage                      | AQLIYA Analyst |
| 0:15–0:25 | **Findings and management decisions** — Summary of findings that were resolved, those still open | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:25–0:35 | **Recommendation** — Recommended next step with rationale                                        | AQLIYA Lead    |
| 0:35–0:45 | **Discussion** — Customer questions, concerns, reactions                                         | Customer       |
| 0:45–0:55 | **Decision** — Customer decides: proceed to MVP, extend pilot, pause, not proceed                | Customer       |
| 0:55–1:00 | **Next steps and close** — Agreed actions, timelines, follow-up                                  | Both           |

**Decisions Needed:**

- Final pilot decision
- If proceeding: commercial terms, timeline for MVP
- If extending: revised scope and duration
- If pausing: conditions and revisit date
- If not proceeding: lessons learned, archive

**Follow-up Actions:**

- Send decision memo and final pilot report
- Execute agreed next steps per commercial follow-up pack
- Archive pilot files

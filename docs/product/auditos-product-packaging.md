# AuditOS Product Packaging

## Product Identity

**Product name:** AuditOS / Financial Intelligence

**Company:** AQLIYA

**Role in architecture:** Current primary product line and first commercial focus under the AQLIYA company platform.

## Official One-Liner

AuditOS helps firms turn trial balances and financial data into draft financial statements, notes, evidence requirements, review findings, and traceable approval workflows.

## Arabic One-Liner

AuditOS يساعد الشركات والمكاتب على تحويل ميزان المراجعة والبيانات المالية إلى مسودات قوائم مالية، إيضاحات، متطلبات أدلة، ملاحظات مراجعة، ومسارات اعتماد قابلة للتتبع.

## Positioning

AuditOS is AQLIYA's governed financial intelligence and audit workflow operating system.

It is designed for teams that still move from trial balance to statements, notes, evidence, review, and approval through fragmented spreadsheets, files, review notes, and manual follow-up.

It does not replace professional judgment. It structures the work around it.

## Product Promise

AuditOS helps teams convert financial inputs into review-ready draft outputs with clearer evidence, stronger traceability, and more controlled approval flow.

## Trust Principle

AI assists. Humans decide. Evidence governs.

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary

1. Audit firms
2. Accounting offices

### Secondary

## Buyer and User Mapping

| Segment | Likely Buyer | Primary Users | Main Need |
|---|---|---|---|
| Audit firms | Managing partner / audit partner | Managers, reviewers, seniors, operators | Controlled review workflow and traceable outputs |
| Accounting offices | Office owner / finance lead | Preparers, reviewers, client-facing accountants | Faster draft preparation with better consistency |
| CFO teams | CFO / finance director | Controllers, reporting teams, finance managers | Better internal reporting preparation and review control |
| SME finance teams | Founder, GM, outsourced finance lead | Small finance team, accountant | Simpler path from raw data to reviewed outputs |

## Core Problem

The path from trial balance to financial statements, notes, evidence, review, and approval is often:

1. Manual
2. Fragmented across files and people
3. Hard to review consistently
4. Hard to trace after the fact
5. Dependent on individual memory and follow-up

## What AuditOS Solves

AuditOS helps teams:

1. Ingest trial balances and related financial data
2. Map accounts into a structured financial model
3. Draft financial statements and notes
4. Define evidence requirements and link supporting review context
5. Create findings and recommendations in a governed workflow
6. Route outputs through review and approval with traceability

## Core Workflow

```text
Engagement
    → Trial Balance
    → Mapping
    → Financial Statements
    → Notes
    → Evidence
    → Findings
    → Review
    → Approval
```

6. Internal finance reporting preparation with governed review

## What AuditOS Is

1. A governed financial intelligence workflow product
2. A structured path from financial data to review-ready outputs
3. A traceability layer for evidence, findings, review, and approval
4. A productivity and control system for regulated financial work

## What AuditOS Is Not

1. Not an autonomous audit opinion generator
2. Not a replacement for licensed professional judgment
3. Not a generic chatbot
4. Not a dashboard-first BI tool
5. Not an ERP replacement
6. Not a CRM
7. Not a black-box AI workflow

**English Headline:**
AuditOS Governs What Excel and ChatGPT Cannot

**English Comparison:**

| Dimension | Excel | ChatGPT | AuditOS |
|---|---|---|---|
| Role | Calculates | Writes text | Governs the financial intelligence workflow |
| Trial balance | Raw cells | Prompt-based | Structured ingestion with validation |
| Account mapping | Manual formulas | Generic suggestions | Governed classification with review override |
| Statements & notes | Manual assembly | Unstructured generation | Drafted within governed templates |
| Evidence requirements | None | None | Structured, linked to accounts |
| Review workflow | Comments, email | None | Built-in review queue with observations |
| Approval trail | None | None | Full traceability from input to sign-off |
| Human judgment | Left to the user | Black-box output | Required at every governance gate |

**Arabic Comparison:**

| البعد | Excel | ChatGPT | AuditOS |
|---|---|---|---|
| الدور | يحسب | يكتب نصوصًا | يحكم مسار الذكاء المالي |
| ميزان المراجعة | خلايا خام | مدخلات نصية | إدخال منظم مع تحقق |
| ربط الحسابات | معادلات يدوية | اقتراحات عامة | تصنيف محكوم مع إمكانية التعديل |
| القوائم والإيضاحات | تجميع يدوي | توليد غير منظم | صياغة ضمن قوالب محكومة |
| متطلبات الأدلة | لا يوجد | لا يوجد | منظمة ومربوطة بالحسابات |
| مسار المراجعة | تعليقات، بريد إلكتروني | لا يوجد | طابور مراجعة مدمج مع ملاحظات |
| مسار الاعتماد | لا يوجد | لا يوجد | تتبع كامل من الإدخال إلى الاعتماد |
| الحكم البشري | متروك للمستخدم | مخرج غير شفاف | مطلوب عند كل بوابة حوكمة |

**English Bottom Line:**
AuditOS does not compete with Excel as a calculator or ChatGPT as a writer. It provides a governed workflow layer that neither can offer — designed specifically for audit and financial reporting.

**Arabic Bottom Line:**

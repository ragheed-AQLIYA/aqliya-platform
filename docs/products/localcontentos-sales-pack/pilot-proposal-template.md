## Pilot Objective

[1–2 sentences describing what the pilot aims to achieve for this specific customer.]

The pilot validates whether a governed local content measurement and evidence review process can:

1. Accept and normalize your procurement data
2. Classify suppliers and spend by local content criteria
3. Support evidence-based review and exception handling
4. Produce a management-ready local content assessment

## Included Work

- Data template preparation and sharing
- Customer instructions and onboarding
- Data intake, validation, and completeness review
- Vendor master classification by locality
- Procurement spend classification and linking
- Contract review (if contracts provided)
- Evidence register setup and confidence review
- Exception identification and documentation
- Findings drafting with recommendations
- Management review session
- Final pilot summary and decision memo
- Pilot closeout meeting

## Excluded Work

- Software access or licenses (product is not implemented)
- Technical integration with customer ERP, HR, or other systems
- Regulatory filing or submission to any authority
- Official local content certification or audit

- Training beyond pilot orientation

---

## Regulatory Boundary

This pilot is an **internal local content governance assessment**. It does not:

- Constitute an official regulatory submission
- Replace statutory local content reporting to any authority
- Represent LCGPA or any regulatory body's endorsement
- Provide binding legal or compliance determinations
- Claim LocalContentOS is implemented as software

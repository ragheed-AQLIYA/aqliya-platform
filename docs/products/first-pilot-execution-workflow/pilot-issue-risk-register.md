# Pilot Issue & Risk Register вЂ” AQLIYA

## Purpose

Track every issue, risk, or concern that arises during a pilot. Prevents items from being forgotten and ensures proper escalation.

## When to Use

- New issue/risk identified during any session, check, or review
- Updated at weekly operating rhythm
- Reviewed before closeout

## Inputs

- Pilot stop conditions (`pilot-control-pack/pilot-stop-conditions.md`)
- Pilot session notes (`pilot-session-notes-template.md`)
- Customer feedback

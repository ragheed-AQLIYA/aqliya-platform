# Pilot Kickoff Agenda — LocalContentOS v0.1

**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Format:** Virtual or in-person
**Attendees:** AQLIYA team, customer sponsor, customer stakeholders

---

## Agenda

| Time   | Topic                              | Lead             | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| ------ | ---------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5 min  | Welcome & context                  | AQLIYA owner     | Introductions, pilot objectives, what success looks like                                                                                                                                                                          |
| 10 min | LocalContentOS overview            | AQLIYA owner     | What LocalContentOS is and is not. Governed local content assessment workspace for supplier, spend, evidence, review, and approval workflows. Commercial truthfulness: this is a pilot, not production, not certified compliance. |
| 10 min | Data boundary & requirements       | AQLIYA technical | Walk through the data request: what data is needed, acceptable formats, required fields, timeline. Answer customer questions about data sensitivity and anonymization.                                                            |
| 10 min | Workflow walkthrough (screenshare) | AQLIYA owner     | Live walkthrough of the seeded demo: dashboard, project, suppliers, spend, evidence, classification, findings, review, approval, reports, audit trail. Focus on the human governance gates.                                       |
| 5 min  | Roles & responsibilities           | AQLIYA owner     | Who does what: customer sponsor, data provider, reviewer, approver, AQLIYA pilot lead.                                                                                                                                            |
| 10 min | Pilot timeline & success criteria  | AQLIYA owner     | Walk through the 2-3 week timeline. Define what "pilot success" means. Discuss exit options (convert, extend, hold, no conversion).                                                                                               |
| 5 min  | Risks & mitigations                | Both             | Review the risk register. Surface customer-specific concerns.                                                                                                                                                                     |
| 5 min  | Next steps & action items          | Both             | Data delivery date, demo scheduling, user account setup, communication channel.                                                                                                                                                   |

## Materials to Prepare

- This onboarding pack printed or shared
- Customer data request document
- Pilot timeline summary
- Demo environment ready with seed data

## Post-Kickoff Actions

| Action                        | Owner    | Due           |
| ----------------------------- | -------- | ------------- |
| Send customer data request    | AQLIYA   | Same day      |
| Set up pilot user accounts    | AQLIYA   | Within 2 days |
| Customer delivers sample data | Customer | Within 1 week |
| Schedule demo session         | Both     | Within 1 week |

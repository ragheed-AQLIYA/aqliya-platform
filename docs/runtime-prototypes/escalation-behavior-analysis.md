# Escalation Behavior Analysis

Escalation was triggered in 1 of 5 sessions. The specific case: evidence conflict with high materiality.

## Findings

- **Escalation was NOT noisy** — only triggered when genuinely needed
- **Reviewer responded appropriately** — investigated the conflict
- **Escalation level (review_required) was appropriate**
- **No evidence of escalation fatigue** (only 1 trigger across 5 sessions)
- **Escalation timing was correct** — not too early, not too late

# AQLIYA Marketing Repositioning — Executive Summary

**Date:** 2026-06-23  
**Audience:** Leadership, product, commercial

---

## What Changed

AQLIYA's public website moved from **repository-oriented messaging** to **institutional value-oriented messaging**.

Visitors now see a **mature institutional operating platform** that solves audit, decision, compliance, and governance problems — not a software project reporting its build status.

---

## The Problem We Fixed

The homepage and navigation already positioned AQLIYA as a platform. But deeper pages (`/products`, `/about`, system detail pages, English routes) exposed internal language:

- L4 / L5 / L6 maturity badges  
- "جاهز للبايلوت", "نشط", "قيد التطوير"  
- Product catalog framing ("3 أنظمة نشطة")  
- Placeholder blocks for future customer proof  

A CEO in 30 seconds could conclude: *"Interesting platform, but still in pilot."*

---

## The Solution

**Two-layer content model:**

1. **Vision layer** (homepage, platform, about, systems, industries, governance)  
   Speaks in outcomes: faster decisions, audit traceability, compliance readiness, governed knowledge, lower risk.

2. **Proof layer** (proof center, executive brief, buyers, engagement models)  
   Speaks in validation: diagnostic session, operational evaluation, evidence-based decision.

Internal engineering truth stays in `PRODUCT_STATUS_MATRIX` and related docs — **untouched**.

# AuditOS Overview

## What is AuditOS?

AuditOS is an official AQLIYA product line focused on financial intelligence and governed audit workflows. It helps audit firms convert raw accounting data into structured financial statement drafts, notes, evidence requirements, review points, and approval workflows.

AuditOS is not an audit tool. It is a governed, evidence-backed, AI-assisted workflow operating system for audit and financial intelligence, built on the principle that AI assists, humans decide, and evidence governs.

## Core Identity

| Aspect | Statement |
|--------|-----------|
| Category | AuditOS / Financial Intelligence product line |
| Role | Assistive — prepares draft outputs for professional review |
| Method | Workflow-first, evidence-centric, governance-first, human-in-the-loop |
| Differentiator | Financial intelligence depth, structural governance, full traceability |

## What AuditOS Does

- Ingests trial balance and financial data
- Maps client chart of accounts to a canonical financial model
- Validates financial data structure and identifies anomalies
- Generates draft financial statements (SFP, P/L, SCE, Cash Flow)
- Generates draft notes to financial statements
- Identifies evidence requirements per account area
- Creates review findings with severity classification
- Routes findings through governed approval workflows
- Publishes approved engagement packages with full traceability

## What AuditOS Does Not Do

- Issue statutory audit opinions
- Replace licensed auditors or accountants
- Guarantee compliance without professional review
- Finalize official financial statements without approval
- Accept evidence autonomously
- Operate as black-box AI
- Replace professional judgment

## Guiding Principles

1. **AI assists. Humans decide. Evidence governs.** Every AI output is a draft requiring human review.
2. **Workflow before dashboard.** The primary experience is the governed workflow.
3. **Governance is structural.** Rules are enforced by the system, not by policy documents.
4. **Evidence is the unit of trust.** Every output must be traceable to its supporting evidence.
5. **AuditOS is a product line, not the company identity.** AQLIYA is the company platform across multiple operating systems.
6. **Financial Intelligence is a core moat.** Domain depth remains the defensible advantage.

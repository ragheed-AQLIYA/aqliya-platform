# Non-Authoritative Disclaimer

## Important Notice

**AuditOS is an assistive financial intelligence and audit preparation system. It is not a substitute for professional judgment, and its outputs are not final or authoritative without review and approval by qualified professionals.**

## Disclaimers

### 1. Draft Nature of Outputs

All financial statements, notes, findings, recommendations, and other outputs generated by AuditOS are **preparation drafts**. They are not final, not approved, and not suitable for distribution as official financial statements or audit reports without:

- Review by a qualified professional accountant or auditor
- Management approval of the financial statements
- Auditor approval of the audit report
- Compliance with applicable regulatory requirements

### 2. No Professional Opinion

AuditOS does not issue audit opinions, review opinions, or any form of assurance conclusion. Professional opinions require the exercise of professional judgment, consideration of entity-specific facts and circumstances, and compliance with professional standards — all of which are exclusively human responsibilities.

### 3. Data Dependency

The quality and completeness of AuditOS outputs depend entirely on the quality and completeness of the data provided. AuditOS does not verify the accuracy of source data. Users are responsible for ensuring that data entered into the system is accurate, complete, and appropriate for the intended use.

### 4. Regulatory Compliance

AuditOS assists in preparing draft outputs that may support compliance with applicable accounting and auditing standards. However, compliance with specific regulatory requirements depends on entity-specific facts, applicable laws and regulations, and professional judgment. AuditOS does not guarantee regulatory compliance.

### 5. AI Limitations

AI-assisted features in AuditOS are designed to assist, not replace, professional judgment. AI outputs may contain errors, omissions, or inaccuracies. All AI-generated outputs require human review and approval before use in any professional capacity.

### 6. Professional Responsibility

The use of AuditOS does not reduce or transfer the professional responsibility of:

- Licensed auditors and accountants
- Management and those charged with governance
- Audit firms and their partners

All professional conclusions, opinions, and decisions remain the sole responsibility of the qualified professionals who make them.

# Professional Disclaimer

## Purpose

This document defines the professional disclaimer that accompanies all AuditOS outputs. It protects the firm, the reviewer, and the client by making the draft nature of all outputs explicit.

## Standard Disclaimer

Every published output from AuditOS includes the following disclaimer:

```txt
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                     IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

This document has been prepared by AuditOS as a draft for
review purposes only. It is not final, not approved, and does not
constitute professional advice, an audit opinion, or a concluded
financial statement.

AuditOS is an assistive financial intelligence system. All
outputs are preparation drafts that require review, supplementation,
and approval by qualified professionals before any official use.

Specifically:

1. Financial statements are drafts and require management approval
   and auditor review before they may be presented as final.

2. Notes to financial statements are drafts and require review
   for completeness, accuracy, and compliance with applicable
   accounting standards.

3. Review findings and recommendations are preliminary and require
   professional assessment of materiality, context, and impact.

4. Evidence requirements are suggestions and do not constitute
   a complete audit program. The auditor remains responsible for
   determining the nature, timing, and extent of audit procedures.

5. AI-assisted features are tools that augment professional
   judgment — they do not replace it. All AI-generated outputs
   require human review and approval.

Core Principle: AI assists. Humans decide. Evidence governs.

AQLIYA builds AI operating systems for business decisions, sales, finance, compliance, and professional reporting.
AuditOS / Financial Intelligence is one official AQLIYA product line, not the full company identity.
Financial Intelligence remains a core capability within that product line.

Generated by:   AuditOS
Date:           [generation date]
Status:         DRAFT — NOT FINAL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

## Disclaimer Placement

| Output | Disclaimer Location |
|--------|-------------------|
| Financial statement draft | Header/footer of every page |
| Notes draft | Header/footer of every page |
| Findings summary | Cover page |
| Recommendation | Before recommendation text |
| Publication package | Cover page |
| Client-facing portal | Persistent banner |
| Exported file | Metadata + cover page |

## Disclaimer by Output Type

### Financial Statement Disclaimer

```txt
DRAFT — NOT FINAL
Prepared by AuditOS on [date]
These financial statements are draft preparation materials.
They require review and approval by qualified professionals.
```

### Notes Disclaimer

```txt
DRAFT — NOT FINAL
Prepared by AuditOS on [date]
These notes are draft disclosures based on available data.
They may require supplementation with entity-specific information.
```

### Finding Disclaimer

```txt
PRELIMINARY — FOR REVIEW
This finding is an AI-assisted draft that requires professional
assessment before it may be included in any engagement output.
```

## Disclaimer Compliance

| Requirement | Enforcement |
|-------------|-------------|
| Every output includes disclaimer | System-generated — cannot be removed |
| Disclaimer is clearly visible | Displayed prominently, not hidden |
| Disclaimer is in appropriate language | Bilingual (Arabic/English) where applicable |
| Disclaimer cannot be modified by user | Protected text, not user-editable |
| Disclaimer persists on all copies | Embedded in exported files |

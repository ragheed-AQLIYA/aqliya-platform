# Evidence by Account Area

## Purpose

This document defines the specific evidence required for each account area in an audit engagement.

## Cash and Cash Equivalents

| Evidence Item | Source | Assertion Coverage |
|--------------|--------|-------------------|
| Bank confirmation | Bank (third-party) | Existence, Rights, Valuation |
| Bank statements | Bank (third-party) | Existence, Completeness |
| Bank reconciliation | Client | Completeness, Valuation |
| List of all bank accounts | Client management | Completeness |
| Cash count sheet | Client/Internal | Existence (petty cash) |
| Restricted cash agreements | Bank/Management | Presentation |
| Foreign currency translation | Bank/System | Valuation |

# Sunbul — Pilot Offer

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-05-18
**Classification:** Confidential — Pilot Client

## What This Pilot Is

This is a **controlled, time-boxed pilot** of Sunbul — a governed operational platform for managing cases, documents, reviews, approvals, and exports with per-client data isolation and full audit trail.

The pilot allows your team to test the complete case workflow with real documents and real reviews, under the supervision of Sunbul operators.

## What This Pilot Is Not

- Not a full production deployment
- Not an AI system
- Not a document analysis or OCR tool
- Not a replacement for your existing systems
- Not a security-audited enterprise platform
- Not a finished product — active development is ongoing

## Pilot Duration

**4 weeks** (adjustable to 2–6 weeks by agreement)

## What Sunbul Will Prove

1. Client data isolation works — no cross-client data leakage
2. Role-based permissions are enforceable — operators create, reviewers approve
3. The case lifecycle covers the team's real workflow from creation to sign-off
4. Document handling is reliable — upload, reference, and download
5. PDF export preserves provenance — who did what, when, based on which document
6. Audit trail provides traceability for compliance

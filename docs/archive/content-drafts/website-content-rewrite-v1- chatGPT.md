## 1. Rewrite Principles

### Platform-first
AQLIYA must always be presented as the parent institutional platform/company, not as AuditOS, not as one product page, and not as a generic SaaS tool.

### Governance-first
Every page should reinforce that value comes from governed workflow, traceability, human review, approval, evidence, and institutional control.

### Arabic-first
Arabic copy must be natural, executive, and precise. English names are kept where they are product names, but output labels and descriptive text should be Arabic-first where the page language is Arabic.

### No unsupported claims
Cloud is available now. Private/On-Prem, Air-Gapped, Local AI, Studio, Institutional Memory, and Model Governance must be described as strategic/future unless implementation proof exists.

### Product boundary clarity
AuditOS is the first proof product. LocalContentOS is the second strategic product. DecisionOS is active/adjacent. SalesOS is prototype/future. SimulationOS is future/marketing-only. Custom Systems are a strategic direction, not a fully implemented Studio product unless verified.

### Human-reviewed / evidence-governed language
Avoid copy that implies autonomous AI decisions. Use language such as: assists, suggests, organizes, drafts, flags, routes, documents, and prepares for human review.

### Primary English Positioning

#### Short one-liner
AQLIYA is a Private Governed Institutional Intelligence Platform for auditable, human-reviewed, evidence-governed workflows.

#### Medium paragraph
AQLIYA helps institutions turn data, workflows, and decisions into governed operating systems. It is not a chatbot, a single product, or SaaS-only positioning; it is a platform layer that connects intelligence, governance, evidence, permissions, review, and auditability inside institutional workflows.

#### Homepage hero version
An institutional platform for intelligence that can be understood, reviewed, and governed. AQLIYA connects data, workflow, permissions, evidence, and human approval into one operating path, so institutions can use intelligence without losing control over decisions, context, or traceability.

**Arabic:** الذكاء يساعد. الإنسان يقرر. الدليل يحكم.

### What AQLIYA Is

- منصة ذكاء مؤسسي خاص ومحكوم.
- بنية تشغيلية تربط الذكاء بالحوكمة وسير العمل والأدلة.

- نواة مشتركة لبناء أنظمة مؤسسية متخصصة.
- Cloud الآن، مع مسار استراتيجي للتشغيل الخاص مستقبلًا.
- نظام يعتمد على المراجعة البشرية وسجل التدقيق وربط المخرجات بالمصادر.

- ليست chatbot للمؤسسة.
- ليست AuditOS فقط.
- ليست SaaS فقط.
- ليست CRM تقليديًا.
- ليست أداة توليد نصوص بلا حوكمة.
- ليست بديلًا عن صاحب القرار أو المراجع أو المسؤول المهني.

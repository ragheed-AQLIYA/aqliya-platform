## Section 4: Customer Communication Scenarios

### Scenario A: Customer asks "Is this an automated audit?"

**Correct response:**
> "No. AuditOS is an assistive system. It helps structure the workflow, but every output requires human review. The AI suggests classifications and drafts — your reviewer validates everything."

**Practice this response:** ☐ Pass ☐ Needs Work

### Scenario B: Customer asks "Can we rely on these financial statements for filing?"

**Correct response:**
> "Not yet. These are drafts. They show you what the system can produce, but they require full human review before any filing or compliance use. The purpose of this pilot is to validate the workflow."

**Practice this response:** ☐ Pass ☐ Needs Work

### Scenario C: Customer asks "What happens if the AI makes a mistake?"

**Correct response:**
> "AI suggestions are flagged with confidence scores. Every suggestion must be accepted or rejected by a human reviewer. Mistakes are caught by the reviewer — that's by design. The system is built around human judgment, not around AI perfection."

**Practice this response:** ☐ Pass ☐ Needs Work

### Scenario D: Customer asks "How is this different from Excel?"

**Correct response:**
> "Excel is a general tool. AuditOS is a structured workflow: your data comes in, gets mapped to a standard chart of accounts, generates statements and notes, links evidence, and creates a full audit trail. Everything is connected and traceable."

**Practice this response:** ☐ Pass ☐ Needs Work

### Scenario E: Customer asks "What do you need from us?"

**Correct response:**
> "We need your Trial Balance file in Excel or CSV format, with account codes, names, and balances. We also need your reviewer to validate the outputs. We'll handle the rest and walk you through everything."

**Practice this response:** ☐ Pass ☐ Needs Work

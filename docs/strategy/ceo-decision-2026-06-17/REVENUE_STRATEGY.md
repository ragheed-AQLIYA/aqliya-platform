## Fastest Path: First Paid Pilot

**Product:** LocalContentOS  
**Buyer:** Saudi contractor, EPC, or industrial company under LC obligations — or their advisory firm  
**Offer:** 90-day **controlled pilot** — workbook import, TB/scoring, ERP connector setup, pilot-readiness review, human-reviewed AI recommendations  

**Price signal:** SAR 150K–400K pilot (operator-assisted setup) — adjust to market; point is **paid**, not free POC  

**Prerequisites (non-negotiable):**
- Week 1: green build, test-token gone
- Week 2: honest commercial claims register
- Week 4: staging demo repeatable in <45 min

**Sales motion:**
1. Lead with **pilot-readiness dashboard** — shows gaps before they buy
2. Demo **workbook + one ERP import** (CSV minimum)
3. Show **evidence grounding** on AI output — trust differentiator
4. Close with **operator-assisted pilot SOW** — not self-serve SaaS fantasy

**Why not AuditOS first:** Equally strong; often longer procurement via audit firms. Run **parallel** outbound — LC often faster in industrial sector.

## Fastest Path: First Enterprise Customer

**Realistic enterprise (180 days):** Large Saudi industrial or holding group — LC program office + optional AuditOS for group audit  

**Not realistic in 90 days:** Full SAML + air-gap + SOC2 — disclose gaps; lead with cloud + local AI option  

**Enterprise wedge:**
- Bilingual Arabic-first operations
- Evidence-based AI (not black box)
- Tenant isolation + audit logs (verified in audit)
- SCIM for IT (single-org limit disclosed)
- Operator-assisted SSO setup

**RFP strategy:** Respond only when LC or audit scope matches — **no generic platform RFPs**

**Gov opportunity:** LocalContentOS aligned with KSA industrial policy — position as **compliance intelligence**, not generic AI. No air-gap promise.

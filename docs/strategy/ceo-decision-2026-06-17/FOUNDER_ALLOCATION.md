## Rationale by area

### Customers (30%) — highest

- LC compliance buyers purchase **trust in founder + governance story**
- Pilot-readiness dashboard is a **conversation tool** — use it live
- First 3 pilots require hand-holding — plan for it
- **Failure mode:** Founder in codebase while pipeline empty

### Product (25%)

- Define **pilot scope document** — what LC pilot includes/excludes
- Weekly "no" list — reject Sales, Studio, new modules
- Review pilot-readiness metrics with eng — not UI polish
- **Not:** Writing specs for 6 products

### Sales (20%)

- Target list: 30 Saudi industrial / EPC / manufacturing LC officers
- Parallel: 10 audit firm partners for AuditOS (secondary)
- Demo script: workbook + ERP + evidence AI — 30 minutes
- **Paid pilot SOW** template ready Week 4
- **Not:** Generic platform marketing

### Engineering (15%)

- **Week 1:** Founder codes or pairs on P0 (test-token, build) — exception
- **Week 2+:** Code review gates only; hire/delegate implementation
- Unblock: schema drift, MFA, honest CI
- **Not:** Sales refactor, new features, architecture essays

### Partnerships (5%)

- One ERP advisory partner (implementation capacity)
- One audit network intro for AuditOS
- **Not:** 5 signed MOUs with no revenue

### Documentation (2%)

- Approve commercial claims register
- Sign off master ref status updates
- **Not:** Documentation sprints

### Hiring (3%)

- Trigger: first LOI signed
- Hire 1: senior full-stack or customer success for pilot delivery
- **Not:** Sales team, AI researchers, doc writers

# What AQLIYA Does NOT Claim

**Status:** Active — attach to every pilot SOW and enterprise conversation  
**Date:** 2026-06-19  
**Authority:** Board Strategic Assessment 360° · LocalContent Product Doctrine · Master Reference  
**Audience:** Founders, sales, legal, customers, investors

## Purpose

Commercial truth protects trust. This document lists capabilities **not** sold, **not** implemented, or **explicitly excluded** from pilot scope. If it is not listed as included in your SOW, assume it is **out of scope**.

---

## English — Platform-Wide Exclusions

| We do NOT claim | What we DO offer instead |
|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Autonomous AI decisions | AI **assists**; humans review and approve |
| Regulator-certified or legally binding outputs | Governed drafts with disclaimers on every export |
| Replacement for licensed audit firms or audit opinions | Audit workspace that **assists** audit teams |
| Official LCGPA certification or regulatory submission | Local content **intelligence** with evidence and review |
| Enterprise-ready / SOC2-certified today | Pilot-ready with documented gaps and roadmap |
| On-Prem, Air-Gapped, or private appliance packages | AQLIYA Cloud (private deployment = strategic future only) |
| Guaranteed 100% AI accuracy | Measured benchmarks in controlled conditions; human review required |
| Real-time AI at unlimited scale | Governed workflows; async processing where needed |
| Full SSO for any IdP without operator setup | SSO with **operator configuration** (SAML/OIDC) |
| CRM or ERP replacement | Domain workflows with **import/connectors** where implemented |
| Seven equal “production products” | **LocalContentOS** and **AuditOS** as primary commercial wedges |
| SalesOS as customer-facing CRM | Internal workspace only — **not sold** in pilot phase |
| RiskOS, SimulationOS, AQLIYA Studio as live products | Audit submodule, marketing redirect, or future concept only |
| Pen test completed / ISO 27001 certified | Scheduled or roadmap — verify status in `AQLIYA_CURRENT_STATE.md` |
| Staging environment parity until verified | Production health verified; staging status per current-state doc |

## Product-Specific Exclusions

### LocalContentOS

- **NOT** an official LCGPA calculation engine with binding legal formulas  
- **NOT** a regulatory certification platform  
- **NOT** a substitute for licensed auditor AUP or government portal submission  
- Exports include disclaimer: internal governance report, not regulator-approved certification  

**IS:** Evidence-linked supplier classification, review/approval workflow, workbook scoring, ERP import (operator setup), bilingual exports, audit trail.

### AuditOS

- **NOT** an audit opinion or signed financial statement certification  
- **NOT** replacement for professional judgment of engagement partner  
- **NOT** guaranteed FS accuracy on all engagements (pilot benchmarks are engagement-specific)  

**IS:** TB upload, mapping assist, FS generation assist, evidence vault, review gates, bilingual export with status disclaimers.

### AI (All Products)

- Default: **deterministic assist** — not “magic AI”  
- Cloud/local LLM: optional, tenant policy, human review before final output  
- Every AI output: suggestion/draft/analysis — never final decision

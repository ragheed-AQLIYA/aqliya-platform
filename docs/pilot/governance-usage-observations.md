# Governance Usage Observations — Under Real Pressure

## Did Reviewer Notice DraftOnlyBanner?
| Engagement | Noticed? | Timing | Notes |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| E1 | Y | Immediate (opening screen) | Banner prominent on load |
| E2 | Y | After 30s | Reviewer focused on data first |
| E3 | Y | Immediate | Multiple banner locations helped |
| E4 | N | Never noticed | Reviewer bypassed to TB worksheet |
| E5 | Y | After ~2 min | Noticed during evidence review |

- **Observation**: DraftOnlyBanner noticed ~80% of time. Missed when reviewer jumps straight to detailed work.

## Governance Fatigue Signs
| Sign | Observed? | Frequency |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Reviewer stops opening panel | Y | After 2nd use in same session |
| Banner ignored after first exposure | Y | Consistent across all engagements |
| Provenance check skipped in high-volume areas | Y | During revenue line-item review |
| Escalation bypassed despite unresolved issue | Y | Reviewer accepted lower certainty |
| Governance referred to as "extra clicks" | Y | Verbal feedback during session |

- **Observation**: Fatigue sets in after ~2 governance interactions per session. Design assumption that governance is lightweight confirmed incorrect under real pressure. Previous phase findings on fatigue hold.

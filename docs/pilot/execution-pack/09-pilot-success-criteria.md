# AQLIYA Pilot — Success Criteria

**Document:** 09-pilot-success-criteria.md  
**Purpose:** Define the criteria for evaluating whether the pilot succeeded.  
**Owner:** Pilot Lead  

---

## Decision Framework

At the end of the pilot, the team will make one of three decisions:

| Decision | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **Go** | Pilot met all success criteria. Proceed to commercial readiness preparation. |
| **Conditional Go** | Pilot met most criteria. Specific gaps identified and documented. Address gaps before commercial readiness. |
| **No-Go** | Pilot did not meet critical criteria. Gaps are fundamental. Re-evaluate product direction before next pilot. |

---

## Section 1: Technical Success

| # | Criteria | Pass/Fail | Evidence Required | Weight |
|---|----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| 1.1 | Trial Balance uploaded successfully | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Intake checklist complete | Critical |
| 1.2 | All accounts mapped (no unmapped) | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | TA from Reviewer QA | Critical |
| 1.3 | Financial statements generated | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | SFP + P&L exist | Critical |
| 1.4 | Statements balance (A = L + E) | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | QA verified | Critical |
| 1.5 | Notes generated for material accounts | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Notes checklist complete | Medium |
| 1.6 | Evidence requirements generated | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Evidence page populated | Medium |
| 1.7 | Traceability chain is functional | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Full trace completed | Critical |

**Technical Go:** All critical items pass.  
**Technical Conditional Go:** All critical items pass, medium items have documented gaps.  
**Technical No-Go:** Any critical item fails.

## Section 2: Workflow Success

|---|----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|

**Workflow Go:** All critical items pass.

## Section 4: Reviewer Trust Success

| # | Criteria | Pass/Fail | Evidence Required | Weight |
|---|----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| 4.1 | Reviewer rates trust ≥ 3/5 | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q2.1 | Critical |
| 4.2 | Reviewer rates AI confidence ≥ 3/5 | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q2.2 | Medium |
| 4.3 | AI vs human distinction is clear | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q2.3 | Critical |
| 4.4 | Reviewer found AI suggestions helpful | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q5.1 | Medium |

**Trust Go:** All critical items ≥ 3/5.  
**Trust Conditional Go:** Trust is ≥ 3/5 but AI confidence < 3.  
**Trust No-Go:** Trust rating < 3.

## Section 5: Customer Value Success

| # | Criteria | Pass/Fail | Evidence Required | Weight |
|---|----------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| 5.1 | Customer found the workflow useful | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q1.1 ≥ 3 | Critical |
| 5.2 | Customer sees value in the system | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q1.3 ≥ 3 | Critical |
| 5.3 | Customer rates overall experience ≥ 3/5 | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q1.2 ≥ 3 | Critical |
| 5.4 | Customer would continue using the system | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q8.1 = Yes/Conditional | Critical |
| 5.5 | Customer would consider paid pilot | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Feedback form Q8.2 = Yes/Maybe | Medium |

**Customer Value Go:** All critical items ≥ 3, Q8.1 = Yes.  
**Customer Value Conditional Go:** All critical items ≥ 3, Q8.1 = Conditional.  
**Customer Value No-Go:** Any critical item < 3, or Q8.1 = No.

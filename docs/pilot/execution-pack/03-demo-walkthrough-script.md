**Purpose:** Script for the live demo walkthrough with the customer.  
**Owner:** Pilot Lead  
**Tone:** Executive, precise, trust-driven, no hype

## Opening Script (3 minutes)

> "Thank you for joining us today. We're going to walk through AuditOS using your Trial Balance data.
>
> AQLIYA is an enterprise systems company. AuditOS is one of our systems — purpose-built for financial intelligence and audit workflows.
>
> Our core principle is: **AI assists. Humans decide. Evidence governs.**
>
> Everything you see today is a draft. Every AI suggestion requires human review. Every output is traceable back to its source.
>
> Our goal today is to show you the workflow: from Trial Balance intake through account mapping, financial statements, evidence, review, and traceability.
>
> We'll pause at each step for your questions."

---

## Section 1: AQLIYA Positioning (2 minutes)

### Talking Points
- AQLIYA is an **Enterprise Systems Builder** — not an audit firm, not a generic AI company
- We build systems around how organizations actually work
- **`عقلية شركة أنظمة مؤسسية متعددة المجالات`**
- AuditOS is our active primary system for financial intelligence

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- "Enterprise Systems Builder" positioning
- No overclaim — we don't replace auditors
- Human review is embedded in the workflow

### Customer Questions to Ask
- "How does this align with your current audit process?"
- "What challenges are you facing with your current workflow?"

---

## Section 2: AuditOS Purpose (3 minutes)

### Talking Points
- AuditOS transforms a Trial Balance into structured, traceable audit outputs
- The workflow: **TB → Mapping → Statements → Notes → Evidence → Findings → Review → Approval**
- Every step is governed — nothing is hidden
- AI assists with suggestions, classifications, and drafting — but **every output is reviewed by a human**

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Draft/Review/Approval status badges visible at every stage
- "Draft · Requires human review · Not final" disclaimers on AI outputs
- Acceptance/Rejection tracking on all AI suggestions

### Demo Actions
- Show the AuditOS workspace overview page
- Point out the workspace status indicator
- Point out the AI indicator and intelligence summary

---

## Section 3: Trial Balance Intake (5 minutes)

### Talking Points
- Your Trial Balance has been received, validated, and uploaded
- We verified: debits = credits, all amounts numeric, no duplicate accounts
- The system has analyzed the structure and is ready for mapping

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Validation results are visible — nothing happens silently
- If there were issues, they would be flagged before proceeding

### Demo Actions
- Show the Trial Balance page with the uploaded data
- Show the total debits, total credits, variance display
- Show the account table with codes, names, and amounts

### Customer Questions to Ask
- "Does the data look correct to you?"
- "Are there any accounts that seem misclassified?"

---

## Section 4: Account Mapping (5 minutes)

### Talking Points
- Accounts from your TB are mapped to a canonical chart of accounts
- AI suggests mappings based on account name, code pattern, and category
- Every AI suggestion is color-coded by confidence
- The reviewer can **accept, reject, or manually override** any mapping
- No mapping is final until a human confirms it

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Confidence scores on every AI suggestion
- All AI suggestions are visually distinct from human-confirmed mappings
- Mapping status: pending / confirmed / rejected — always visible

### Demo Actions
- Show the mapping page with AI-suggested mappings
- Accept one AI suggestion
- Manually override one mapping
- Show the status change after confirmation

### Customer Questions to Ask
- "How do you currently handle account classification?"
- "Would AI-suggested mappings save your team time?"

---

## Section 5: Financial Statements (5 minutes)

### Talking Points
- Once mappings are confirmed, financial statements are generated automatically
- Statement of Financial Position, Statement of Profit or Loss, and supporting schedules
- Every line item is linked back to its source account
- Draft status is clearly shown — none of this is final

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Every statement line is traceable to a mapped account
- Draft watermark is visible
- Statements are rebuilt automatically when mappings change

### Demo Actions
- Show the Statements page
- Navigate to Statement of Financial Position
- Click through a line item to see the source accounts
- Show the draft status badge

---

## Section 6: Notes Generation (3 minutes)

### Talking Points
- AI drafts notes/disclosures based on account classifications and balances
- Notes are templates — each one requires review and customization
- Missing information is flagged for the reviewer

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- AI-drafted notes are clearly labeled as drafts
- Missing information checklist is visible

### Demo Actions
- Show the Notes page
- Show an AI-drafted note with the "AI Draft" badge
- Show the missing information flags

---

## Section 7: Evidence Requirements (3 minutes)

### Talking Points
- The system identifies evidence needed for each account balance and note
- Evidence items are tracked: missing → requested → uploaded → linked → reviewed → accepted/rejected
- Every piece of evidence is linked to specific accounts and findings

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Evidence state progression is fully tracked
- Evidence links are bidirectional — you can see what a finding's evidence is, and what accounts a piece of evidence supports

### Demo Actions
- Show the Evidence page
- Show the evidence state for a specific account

---

## Section 8: Findings & Review (5 minutes)

### Talking Points
- Findings are generated based on mapping anomalies, missing evidence, and review flags
- Each finding has: severity, materiality, status, and related accounts
- Review comments are tracked with actor, timestamp, and resolution

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- No finding is resolved silently — all actions are logged
- Severity and materiality are clearly visible
- Related evidence and accounts are linked

### Demo Actions
- Show the Findings page
- Show a finding with its related accounts and evidence
- Show a review comment thread

---

## Section 9: Traceability (5 minutes)

### Talking Points
- Every output in AuditOS can be traced back through the full chain
- **TB Line → Mapped Account → Statement Line → Note → Evidence → Finding → Approval**
- This is the core of our trust principle: **Evidence governs.**

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Full audit trail is visible at every stage
- AI decisions are tracked separately from human decisions
- Every status change is recorded with actor and timestamp

### Demo Actions
- Show the Traceability page
- Start from a finding and trace back to the TB line
- Show the audit trail for a specific action

---

## Section 10: Approval (2 minutes)

### Talking Points
- The approval workflow ensures nothing moves forward without explicit human authorization
- Approvals are tracked with before/after snapshots
- No output is "live" until approved

### Trust Signals to Highlight
- Approval records include the approver's rationale
- Before/after comparison is available
- Immutable snapshot on approval

---

## Section 11: Human Review Disclaimer (2 minutes)

### Talking Points
> "Let me be very clear about what AuditOS does and does not do.
>
> **AuditOS assists. It does not replace.**
>
> Every output is a **draft** that requires human review.
> Every AI suggestion must be **accepted or rejected** by a qualified professional.
> Every decision is **traceable** — there is no black box.
>
> This is not a fully automated audit. It is a structured workflow that makes audit work more transparent, more organized, and more traceable."

## What NOT to Say

| Don't Say | Instead Say |
|-----------|-------------|
| "The system audits automatically" | "The system assists with structured workflows" |
| "AI makes the decisions" | "AI suggests, humans decide" |
| "No human review needed" | "Every output requires human review" |
| "Production-ready" | "This is a controlled pilot" |
| "Fully automated" | "Structured and governed" |
| "Replace your auditor" | "Support your audit team" |

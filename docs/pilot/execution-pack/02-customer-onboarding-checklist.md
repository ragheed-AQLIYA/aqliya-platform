## Section 6: Customer Expectations

Confirm the following with the customer before starting:

- [ ] Customer understands this is a **controlled pilot**, not a production system
- [ ] Customer understands **outputs are draft** and require human review
- [ ] Customer understands **AI suggestions are assistive**, not definitive
- [ ] Customer agrees to the pilot timeline and milestones
- [ ] Customer has identified their reviewer(s)
- [ ] Customer knows how to provide feedback
- [ ] Customer knows how to escalate issues

## Section 7: Limitation Disclaimer

Share this disclaimer with the customer:

> **AuditOS — Pilot Program**
>
> This pilot is a controlled evaluation of AuditOS using the customer's data. 
> All outputs are draft and require human review by a qualified professional.
> AI suggestions are assistive and do not replace professional judgment.
> This is not a production system and should not be used for final reporting or compliance purposes.
> By participating, the customer acknowledges these limitations.

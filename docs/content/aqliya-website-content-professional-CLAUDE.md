```

  • ما هي الآثار المحتملة؟

Every detected deviation comes with full explanation:
  • What was detected?
  • Which standard/policy is violated?
  • What data led to this detection?
  • What are the potential implications?
```

```
  • لماذا اتخذ هذا القرار؟
  • متى تم اتخاذه؟
  • ما هي النتيجة؟

Humans take action: Correct, approve exception, or escalate.
Every action is documented with:
  • Who decided?
  • Why this decision?
  • When was it taken?
  • What was the outcome?
```

### 1. Is AQLIYA investable?

| For | Against |
|-----|---------|

| L5-class AuditOS + LocalContentOS depth | Portfolio sprawl signals focus risk |

**Investor terms should require:** 2 paid pilots within 6 months, pen test scheduled, SalesOS freeze documented, monthly CURRENT_STATE updates.

**Investability score: 62/100** — Technical asset yes; commercial proof no.

### 2. Is AQLIYA commercially viable?

**Not yet proven; structurally viable in 12 months with focus.**

Viability requires:
- SAR 1M+ ARR run-rate (3 customers) — achievable per RESOURCE_AND_ROI_PLAN
- Repeatable pilot SOW → paid motion
- Reference case study

**Commercial viability score: 55/100 today → 75/100 target Q2 2027**

### 3. What should be sold first?

**LocalContentOS** to Saudi enterprises with local content obligations.

**Second:** **AuditOS** to regional audit firms / in-house audit (Shalfa-class proof).

**Never lead with:** Platform, SalesOS, AI chat, or multi-product suite.

### 4. What should be stopped immediately?

1. **SalesOS feature development** — freeze 12 months
2. **New product surfaces** — Studio, ComplianceOS, GovOS, RiskOS brand
3. **Enterprise/Government RFP pursuit** — until commercial-ready
4. **On-Prem / Air-Gap sales claims**
5. **Any sprint without LC or Audit customer linkage**

See `ANTI_ROADMAP_2026.md`.

### 7. What is the biggest existential risk?

**Focus risk (R-01):** Building an impressive multi-product platform while failing to convert any product into paying reference customers.

**Secondary:** Commercial risk (R-02) — zero LOIs by Q3 2026.

### 8. What is the single highest leverage action?

**Sign and deliver LocalContentOS Pilot #1 with a named Saudi enterprise in the next 90 days.**

Everything else—pen test, staging, SalesOS merge, SOC2—is in service of repeating that motion, not replacing it.
